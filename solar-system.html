Solar System in Blender

A scientifically accurate 3D model with realistic planetary motion

Project Overview

This Blender project recreates our solar system with attention to scientific accuracy while maintaining artistic quality. All planetary textures are based on NASA imagery, and orbital mechanics follow real-world physics.

The project includes:

  • All 9 planets with accurate relative sizes
  • Realistic orbital paths and speeds
  • Dynamic lighting from the sun
